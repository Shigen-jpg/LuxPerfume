Midnight Orchid

A nocturnal symphony of rare blossoms

Midnight Orchid captures the mysterious allure of rare nocturnal blooms. This exquisite fragrance opens with bright bergamot and pink pepper, evolving into a heart of black orchid and jasmine sambac. The base notes of vanilla bourbon, amber, and Haitian vetiver create a lasting impression of luxury and sophistication.

Midnight Orchid draws inspiration from the rare Black Orchid, which blooms only under the cover of darkness. Our master perfumer has captured this elusive flower's essence through a meticulous extraction process that preserves its mysterious character and depth.


Created as part of our Botanical Nocturne collection, this fragrance celebrates the hidden beauty that emerges after sunset. The unique composition balances rich floral notes with warm, sensual undertones that evolve throughout the evening.


Each bottle is hand-finished and individually numbered, reflecting our commitment to artisanal craftsmanship. The flacon's design echoes the dark elegance of its namesake flower, with hand-polished obsidian glass and a cap adorned with our signature gold emblem.
