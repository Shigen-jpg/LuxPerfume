Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury and Craftsmanship

Founded in 2010 by master perfumer Isabella Laurent, Luxe Essence was born from a passion for artisanal perfumery and a commitment to creating extraordinary scents that evoke emotion and memory.

What began as a small atelier in Paris has grown into an internationally recognized brand synonymous with luxury, innovation, and uncompromising quality. Despite our growth, we maintain the same dedication to craftsmanship that defined our earliest creations.

Each Luxe Essence fragrance is meticulously formulated in our Paris laboratory, where tradition meets innovation. Our perfumers work with only the finest raw materials sourced from around the world—from hand-picked jasmine in Grasse to rare sandalwood from Mysore.

Our Philosophy

The Art of Perfumery

At Luxe Essence, we believe that a fragrance is more than just a scent—it's an expression of individuality, a sensory journey, and an invisible accessory that leaves an unforgettable impression.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that true luxury lies in authenticity, exceptional quality, and attention to detail. We reject the notion of mass production, instead embracing the time-honored traditions of French perfumery while incorporating contemporary innovation.

Every bottle of Luxe Essence represents countless hours of dedication to the art and science of perfume making. From concept to creation, each fragrance undergoes rigorous development and refinement before it bears our name.

OUR VALUES

☘

Sustainability

We are committed to ethical sourcing practices and environmentally responsible production methods. From our recyclable packaging to our support of sustainable farming communities, we strive to minimize our footprint while maximizing our positive impact.

✚

Craftsmanship

We honor the centuries-old tradition of French perfumery through meticulous attention to detail and uncompromising standards. Each fragrance is carefully composed, aged, and bottled by our team of skilled artisans who share our passion for excellence.

❀

Innovation

While respecting tradition, we continuously push the boundaries of perfumery through innovative techniques and unexpected combinations. Our research laboratory is dedicated to discovering new extraction methods and olfactory experiences.

OUR TEAM

Behind every Luxe Essence creation is a team of passionate individuals who share a commitment to excellence. From master perfumers to packaging designers, each person plays a vital role in bringing our vision to life.

OUR JOURNEY

2010

The Beginning

Luxe Essence is founded in Paris by Isabella Laurent. The first collection of five signature fragrances is launched to critical acclaim.

2013

International Expansion

Following success in Europe, Luxe Essence opens its first international boutique in New York City.

2015

Award-Winning Innovation

Our Midnight Orchid fragrance receives the prestigious FiFi Award for innovative composition and design.

2018

Sustainable Initiatives

Launch of our Eco-Conscious Collection featuring sustainable packaging and responsibly sourced ingredients.

2022

Global Recognition

Luxe Essence becomes one of the top 10 luxury fragrance houses worldwide, with boutiques in 15 countries.

2025

Innovation Lab

Opening of our state-of-the-art perfume innovation laboratory dedicated to exploring new frontiers in fragrance creation.
